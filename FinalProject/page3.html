Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups White Sugar
  • 1 1/4 Cups Karo Syrup
  • 1 3/4 Cups Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1 Box Rice Krispies
  • 1 Package Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Package Butterscotch Chips

Directions

  1. Bring the sugar and syrup to a boil.
  2. Take off the heat and mix in 1 1/4 cups of the peanut butter.
  3. Once combined, add in the box of rice crispies and mix.
  4. Spread mixture onto 18x13 cookie sheet and press into even layer.
  5. Melt the chocolate and butterscotch chips together with the remaining peanut butter.
  6. Spread evenly over the rice krispies.
  7. Refridgerate until hardened.
  8. Cut into squares.
