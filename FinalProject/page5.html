Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 3/4 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter
  • 1/2 Cup White Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 TSP Vanilla
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1/2 TSP Baking Soda
  • 1/4 TSP Salt
  • 1 Cup Oatmeal
  • Mini Peanut Butter Cups

Directions

  1. Beat butter and peanut butter till light and fluffy.
  2. Cream mixture with white and brown sugar.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla, and mix well.
  4. Sift in flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix till just combined.
  5. Stir in oatmeal
  6. Refriderate dough until firm.
  7. Preheat oven to 350°F
  8. Grease 1 1/2in muffin tins.
  9. Roll dough into 1 inch balls.
  10. Bake 9-11 minutes.
  11. Remove from oven and press peanut butter cup into the center.
  12. Cool on parchment paper.
